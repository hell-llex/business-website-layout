Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains the use of cookies and similar technologies by transitflow.com ("Company," "we," "us," and "our") to identify you when you visit our website at transitflow.com ("Website"). It details what these technologies are, their purpose, and your rights to manage their usage.

At times, we may employ cookies to gather personal information, or information that becomes personal when combined with other data.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Website owners use cookies to make their websites work efficiently and provide reporting data.

Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, transitflow.com) are known as "first-party cookies." Cookies set by other entities are called "third-party cookies." Third-party cookies enable features like advertising, interactive content, and analytics to function on or through the website. The entities that set these third-party cookies can recognize your computer when it visits the website in question and other sites.

Why do we use cookies?

We use both first- and third-party cookies for various reasons. Some are essential for our Website to function, referred to as "essential" or "strictly necessary" cookies. Others help us track and tailor the interests of our users to enhance the user experience on our online properties. Third parties may also use cookies for advertising, analytics, and other purposes, as explained below.

How can you control cookies?

You have the right to choose whether to accept or reject cookies. The Cookie Consent Manager allows you to specify which cookie categories you accept or reject. Essential cookies cannot be refused as they are necessary to provide services.

The Cookie Consent Manager can be found in the notification banner and on our website. If you opt to decline cookies, you can still use our website, though access to certain features and areas may be limited. You can also configure your web browser settings to accept or reject cookies.
